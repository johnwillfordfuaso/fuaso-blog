        <hi class="Title">Historical Perspective on computing</hi>
        <p class="content">
            The evolution of computing is a fascinating adventure that spans species. Transforming how we live, work, and communicate. As time passes, computers evolves, and tools are developed to make it easier to solve specific situations or difficulties.
We used to utilize abacuses for simple arithmetic calculations, but as time passed, our instruments evolved into mechanical tools, which helped us develop analytical thinking.


        </p> 
        <p class="content">
            i'll give you the example of highlights key milestones of computing

2400 BCE Abacus from Mesopotamia, one of the first instruments for mathematics.
1945 ENIAC the first general-purpose computer that was entirely electrical and used vacuum tubes to perform quick calculations.
1985 Microsoft Windows The introduction of Windows GUI has improved the usability of personal computing.

Global Connectivity: The internet, powered by computation, has connected billions of people around the world. Social media sites, email, and instant messaging have made communication faster and easier.
Digital communication Platforms such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Slack have transformed how we connect, particularly in professional contexts, by enabling remote work and virtual collaboration.
Online learning platforms such as Coursera, Khan Academy, and edX have made education more accessible, allowing students to learn from top universities regardless of their location.
Job Creation and Destruction while computing has provided new job prospects in the technology industry, it has also resulted in the automation of certain jobs.
